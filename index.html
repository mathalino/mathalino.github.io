Frequently Asked Questions

Sermorelin is a growth hormone-releasing peptide that stimulates the pituitary gland to produce and release more natural growth hormone. This helps enhance muscle growth, improve energy levels, support fat loss, and promote overall vitality.

The benefits of Sermorelin include increased lean muscle mass, improved energy and stamina, enhanced recovery from exercise and injuries, better sleep quality, fat loss, and improved skin and bone health

Sermorelin therapy, such as improved sleep, energy, and mood, are often noticeable within 1–2 months, with more significant benefits like muscle tone and fat reduction appearing over 3–6 months. (Molitch, M. E., et al., 2006) (Walker, R. F., & Grundy, S., 1999).

Sermorelin is generally considered safe when used under medical supervision. It stimulates the natural release of growth hormone (GH) and is less likely to cause significant side effects compared to synthetic GH therapy. Common mild side effects may include injection site reactions (redness, swelling, or discomfort), headaches, dizziness, or flushing. Rare side effects could include nausea or an allergic reaction. A systematic review noted that sermorelin's safety profile is favorable due to its mechanism of action, which avoids excessive GH levels. (Source: Walker, R. F., & Grundy, S. (1999). Sermorelin: A Novel Approach to Growth Hormone Therapy. Journal of Anti-Aging Medicine, 2(3), 229-240.) However, individual risks depend on factors like medical history and dosage, so consulting a healthcare provider is essential. (Source: Molitch, M. E., Clemmons, D. R., & Malozowski, S. (2011). Evaluation and Treatment of Adult Growth Hormone Deficiency: An Endocrine Society Clinical Practice Guideline. The Journal of Clinical Endocrinology & Metabolism, 96(6), 1587-1609.)

Sermorelin can be taken by sublingual tablet or injected subcutaneous and typically used 5 to 7 times a week at night before bed but Everlife's Dr's will consult on this and provide exact directions based on your medical history.
